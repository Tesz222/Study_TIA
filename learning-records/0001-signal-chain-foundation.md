# Photodetection Signal Chain Foundation

Established foundational understanding of the complete optical-to-digital signal chain: photodiode (light to current), transimpedance amplifier (current to voltage), and ADC (analog to digital). The user demonstrated ability to calculate I<sub>PD</sub> from responsivity and optical power, compute TIA output voltage, and map voltage to ADC digital code.

**Evidence**: Completed Lesson 0001 with parameter calculation exercises.
**Implications**: Ready for deeper TIA analysis topics — stability compensation, noise optimization, and practical PLC analog input module design.
